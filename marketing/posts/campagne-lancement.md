## Reddit — r/MapPorn (ou r/InternetIsBeautiful)

**Titre :** I made a free browser tool that turns any place on Earth into a vintage-style 3D relief map

**Corps :**
Search a place, watch the terrain rise as a museum-style relief model — with
volumetric clouds, glass lakes, sunset lighting, and country cutouts that sit
on a little plate like the classic shaded-relief posters.

It's free and runs in the browser (desktop/tablet): https://shibumap.com

Fair warning: I optimized for *pretty*, not for survey-grade accuracy. It's an
art piece you can play with.

It started as a fork of kaolti's lovely open-source monolith-terrain
(https://github.com/kaolti/monolith-terrain) — all credit for the foundation
goes to them.

It's brand new, so I'm hunting bugs and ideas: if something breaks or you wish
it did something, I genuinely want to hear it.
